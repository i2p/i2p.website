## Join the Conversation {#chat}

Connect to I2P's IRC channels directly from your browser. Join discussions about I2P development, get support, or chat with the community.

**Available channels:**

- **#i2p** - General I2P discussion and help
- **#i2p-chat** - Off-topic conversations
- **#i2p-dev** - Development discussions
