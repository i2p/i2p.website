too little payoff for the work? 17:09 <jrandom> why would that be better than just having multiple tunnels and sending the message through one of the multiple tunnels? 17:10 <jrandom> (and, erm, wouldn't it be worse, from a security perspective, and anonymity perspective) 17:10 <Raccoon23> well the idea is that nodes could not tell which traffic was part of one tunnel, so that if you were running i2phex and and eepsite, and choose the same hosts for your tunnels, the traffic from the two would be blended as far as the hops could see 17:11 <Raccoon23> which should make timing attacks harder 17:11 <jrandom> ah, yikes, yeah. that adds Really Bad linkability 17:11 <jrandom> its why we moved to per-client tunnel pools in 0.4 17:11 <Raccoon23> explain?
